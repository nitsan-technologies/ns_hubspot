============
Introduction
============

EXT:ns_hubspot integrates TYPO3 forms with HubSpot CRM to automatically capture leads from your website. Our integration ensures that lead information collected through TYPO3 forms is instantly and accurately synchronized with your HubSpot CRM database.

What does it do?
================

EXT:ns_hubspot provides seamless integration between TYPO3 forms and HubSpot CRM, enabling automatic lead capture and synchronization. This extension ensures that all form submissions from your TYPO3 website are automatically transferred to your HubSpot account, maintaining data consistency and improving your lead management workflow.

Features
========

*   Seamless integration with HubSpot CRM
*   Automatic lead synchronization from TYPO3 forms
*   Support for both free and premium versions
*   Easy configuration through TYPO3 backend
*   Real-time data transfer to HubSpot
*   Comprehensive form field mapping
